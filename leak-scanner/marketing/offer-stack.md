# Offer stack

## Free layer (lead generation)
**Free Revenue Leak Scan** — full score + five categories + top 3 leaks +
first-week roadmap + Greenstar service match. Email required for the report
link. Value promise: "know your number in 60 seconds."

## MRR layer (the app)
| Plan | Price | Promise |
|---|---|---|
| Starter | $19/mo | "Never wonder if your site regressed" — monthly re-scans, history, email reports |
| Growth | $49/mo | "Outpace the shop down the street" — weekly re-scans, competitor comparison, full roadmap, PDF export |
| Pro | $99/mo | "All locations, one dashboard" — 5 businesses, priority alerts, monthly strategy call |

Positioning: monitoring is insurance for money already spent on the website.
Anchor: "less than one lost job per year."

## Services layer (the real revenue — sold via reports)
| Service | Anchor scope | Typical trigger |
|---|---|---|
| Website Conversion Rebuild | landing page or full site rebuild | conversion score < 50 |
| CRM & Follow-Up Automation | instant-reply + missed-call text-back + pipeline | follow-up score < 50 |
| Review & Reputation System | review generation flow + on-site proof | trust score < 50 |
| Local SEO Foundation | service/location pages + schema + NAP cleanup | local score < 50 |
| AI Visibility Upgrade | FAQ/differentiator/process content structure | AI score < 50 |
| Lead Reactivation Campaign | past-customer win-back sequence | no email capture + established business |
| Booking / Quote Funnel | booking flow + quote form + confirmation path | no booking/quote path detected |

Bundle logic: every service engagement includes a Growth subscription free
for the engagement duration — the re-scan proves the work moved the score,
and the subscription survives the engagement.

## Ladder
Free scan → (self-serve) Starter/Growth → (assisted) one service →
(retained) service + Pro monitoring → referrals ("forward this report to a
business owner you know").

## Guarantees we DO make
- The report reflects exactly what the scanner detected — transparent methodology.
- Fixed-scope, fixed-price service engagements.
- Re-scan after every engagement; the score movement is visible to the client.

## Claims we NEVER make
- Guaranteed revenue, lead counts, or rankings (search or AI).
- "#1 on Google/ChatGPT" promises.
- Fake scarcity or invented testimonials.
