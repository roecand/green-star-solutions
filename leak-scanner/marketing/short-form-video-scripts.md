# Short-form video scripts (30–60s)

## Script 1: "The 34/100 roofer" (screen recording + voiceover)
**Hook (0–3s):** I ran a roofer's website through our Revenue Leak Scanner. 34 out of 100.
**Body (3–40s):** [screen: report] Watch this. Website Conversion — 50: there's a
quote button, but the site never says where they work. Local Visibility — 50:
no service-area page, no city in the headline. AI Visibility — 20: if you
asked an AI "best roofer near me," this site gives it almost nothing to work
with. Trust — 35: they've been in business 12 years and the website mentions
it nowhere. Follow-up — 35: one form, no response promise, nothing after hours.
**Close (40–55s):** None of this needs more traffic. It needs the leaks fixed.
Scan's free — link in bio.

## Script 3: "The after-hours leak" (talking head + b-roll)
**Hook:** Half your leads show up when you're closed. Where do they go?
**Body:** If the answer is "nowhere" — no form, no booking link, no text-back —
those people bought from whoever answered first the next morning. The scan's
Follow-Up Readiness score shows exactly what your after-hours funnel looks like.
**Close:** Run the free scan. Fix the leak once. It works every night after.

**Hook:** This is what an 88 looks like next to a 14. Same industry.
**Body:** [demo reports side by side] The 88: phone in the header, quote form,
reviews on the page, city everywhere, FAQ, booking link. The 14: "Welcome to
