# Demo report walkthrough script (~3 minutes)

Use on sales calls, screen recordings, or in-person. Open the prospect's real
report if you have one; otherwise `/report/demo-roofer-report` (49/100 — the
most relatable score band).

## 1. The score (30s)
"This is your Revenue Leak Score — one number for how well your online
presence turns interest into calls and bookings. You're at {{score}}. The
verdict under it is plain English: {{verdict}}. Everything below explains
where that number comes from — nothing here is a black box."

## 2. The five cards (60s)
"Five categories. Green is working, red is leaking.
- **Website Conversion** — can a ready-to-buy visitor act in seconds?
- **Local Visibility** — is it obvious where you work?
- **AI Visibility** — honest framing: we can't see AI's rankings, nobody can.
  We measure whether your public content gives answer engines enough clear
  signal to understand and recommend you.
- **Trust & Proof** — would a skeptic believe you?
- **Follow-Up Readiness** — what happens to a lead after hours?

Each card shows the top issue and the fix. Notice it says 'not detected' —
we only report what we actually checked."

## 3. Top leaks + roadmap (45s)
"These are your biggest leaks, worst first, in plain English — this is the
part owners forward to their business partner. Then the roadmap splits fixes
into this week, this month, later. You could hand this list to any competent
web person and they'd know what to do."

## 4. The Greenstar close (30s)
"Every leak maps to one of our services — that's this section. So the
conversation isn't 'hire us for marketing,' it's 'here are the three specific
leaks, here's what fixing them involves, pick where to start.' If you'd
rather fix them yourself, the report is yours either way. Want me to put
together the fix plan?"

## Objection quick-answers
- **"Is this AI making stuff up?"** — Scores come from deterministic checks of
  your actual pages; the AI only writes the explanations from those findings.
  Where data's missing it says "not detected."
- **"My web guy handles this."** — Perfect, forward him the report. Re-scan is
  free after he's done; the score should move.
- **"How do you know it costs me revenue?"** — We don't claim a dollar figure.
  We show the specific friction points; you know your close rate and job value
  better than anyone.
