# Cold DM templates (IG/Facebook/LinkedIn)

Shorter than email; conversational; never paste a link before they reply.

## 1. Permission ask (default)
Hey {{first_name}} — ran your website through a leak scanner we built (checks
why visitors don't turn into calls). Found a couple things worth knowing.
Want me to send the report? Free, no strings.

## 2. Specific observation
Hey! Was on {{business_name}}'s site earlier — noticed there's no way to
book or request a quote after hours. We built a free scanner that catches
stuff like that (it found a few more things). Want the report?

## 3. Local operator angle
Hey {{first_name}}, fellow {{city}} business here. We built a free tool that
scans local businesses' websites for "revenue leaks" — buried phone numbers,
no review proof, sites AI can't understand. Ran yours out of curiosity and
the results are interesting. Want a look?

## 4. After they reply "sure"
Here you go: {{report_link}}
The score's {{score}}/100 — the two biggest leaks are {{leak_1}} and
{{leak_2}}. If you want, I'll walk you through it on a quick call this week,
zero pitch unless you ask: {{booking_link}}

## 5. Comment-to-DM (from content)
You asked about {{industry}} — the most common leak we find is {{common_leak}}.
If you want, I'll run your site through the scanner for free. What's the URL?

## Don'ts
- No walls of text, no "Dear business owner", no fake compliments.
- Never claim their revenue numbers or AI rankings — only what the scan detected.
- If they say no: "No worries — the free scan's at {{app_url}} if you're ever curious." Then stop.
