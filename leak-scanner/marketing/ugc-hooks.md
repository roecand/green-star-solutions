# UGC hooks (30+)

Talking-head / voiceover openers for short-form. Pair each with a screen
recording of a real (or demo) report.

## Leak-awareness
1. Your website might be leaking leads and you would never know.
2. Most small businesses don't need more traffic. They need to stop wasting the traffic they already have.
3. This free scan shows where your business is losing calls.
4. Your competitors aren't better than you. Their websites just leak less.
5. Every day your website has no clear next step, you're paying for visitors who leave.
6. You spent $3,000 on the website. When's the last time you checked if it actually produces calls?
7. Traffic is a vanity metric. Calls are a sanity metric.
8. The most expensive leak in your business is the one you can't see.

## Curiosity / diagnostic
9. I scanned this local business and found 7 reasons customers were not calling.
10. I ran a random plumber's website through our leak scanner. It scored 34 out of 100. Here's why.
11. Grade your own website in 60 seconds — most owners can't get past 60/100.
12. Here's what a 90/100 local business website looks like — and what the 40s all get wrong.
13. POV: you find out your "great" website has been costing you bookings all year.
14. I scan local business websites for a living. These are the 3 leaks I see every single week.

## AI angle (honest framing)
15. AI search is coming for local business. Is your company even understandable?
16. When someone asks ChatGPT for "the best roofer near me," could it even figure out what you do?
17. If your website can't explain your business to a human in 5 seconds, AI has no chance either.
18. Your next customer might not Google you. They might ask an AI. Is your site ready for that?
19. FAQ pages aren't boring anymore — they're how answer engines learn to recommend you.

## Specific-leak hooks
20. If your website has no clear CTA, you are paying for visitors who leave.
21. Your phone number is three clicks deep. That's not a website, that's a maze.
22. No reviews on your homepage = strangers have zero reasons to pick you.
23. Half of your leads come in after hours. Where do they go if you have no form?
24. "We'll get back to you soon" is where leads go to die.
25. Your website says "Welcome to our website." Your competitor's says "Get a free quote today." Guess who gets the call.
26. A missed call with no text-back isn't a missed call. It's a donated customer.

## Social-proof / journey
27. I fixed the top 3 leaks on my own site and watched the calls change. Here's the before and after scan.
28. We scanned 25 local businesses in Las Vegas. The average score was under 60. Here's the most common leak.
29. Business owner asked why the phone stopped ringing. The scan answered in 60 seconds.
30. This is the report I send business owners before we ever get on a call.

## Challenge / interactive
31. Comment your industry and I'll show you the most common leak we find in it.
32. Bet your website can't score above 70. Free scan, link in bio. Prove me wrong.
33. Run the scan before your competitor does — because one of you will.
