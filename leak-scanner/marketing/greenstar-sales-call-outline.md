# Strategy call outline (20 minutes)

Goal: agree on the top 2–3 leaks and the first engagement — not to pitch
everything.

## Before the call (5 min prep)
- Open the lead in `/admin/leads/{{id}}` — score, findings, severity list.
- Note top 3 recommendations and which Greenstar services they map to.
- Check lifecycle: did they request help (warm) or is this outreach (colder)?

## 1. Frame (2 min)
"I'll walk your report, you tell me what's actually true about your business,
we agree on the two or three highest-leverage fixes. If we do them, great; if
you do them yourself, the plan's yours."

## 2. Their reality (5 min) — talk less, ask:
- Where do most jobs come from today?
- What happens when someone calls and you're on a job? (missed-call leak)
- Who answers form leads, how fast? (follow-up leak)
- When did the phone last feel slow? What did you change before that?

## 3. Report walk (6 min)
Use the demo-report script structure: score → the 2 worst categories →
top leaks → roadmap. Tie every finding to their answers from step 2:
"You said calls go to voicemail on jobs — that's this Follow-Up score."

## 4. Prescription (4 min)
Present ONE primary service + one supporting:
- Score < 40 → Website Conversion Rebuild (foundation first)
- 40–65 + weak follow-up → CRM & Follow-Up Automation
- 40–65 + weak trust → Review & Reputation System
- 65+ → Local SEO Foundation or AI Visibility Upgrade (edge sharpening)
Give scope in plain terms: what's done in week 1, what's done by week 4.

## 5. Close (3 min)
"Fastest path: we start with {{primary_service}}, re-scan in 30 days, and the
score tells us both if it worked. Want me to send the proposal today?"
- Yes → mark `proposal_sent`, send same day.
- Maybe → book the follow-up on the call; mark `contacted`; send recap + report.
- No → "The monitoring plan will tell you when it's time" (Starter/Growth pitch).

## After
Update lifecycle status + admin note immediately. Every hot lead gets a next
step with a date — no lead sits in "contacted" for more than a week.
