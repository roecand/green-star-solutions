## Problem section
**Header:** Traffic is expensive. Missed leads are invisible.
Most local businesses don't need more visitors — they need to stop wasting
the ones they already have. A buried phone number, a slow reply, a site AI
can't understand: each one quietly sends ready-to-buy customers to a
competitor. You never see it happen.

Three columns:
- **Visitors leave** — No clear next step above the fold means ready buyers bounce without calling.
- **Leads go cold** — Form submissions that wait hours for a reply usually buy from whoever answered first.
- **AI skips you** — Answer engines recommend businesses they can understand. Thin content makes you invisible.

## What the scanner checks
Five categories, dozens of deterministic checks, one clear score.
1. **Website Conversion** — CTAs, phone visibility, forms, mobile, offer clarity.
2. **Local Visibility** — city signals, service areas, NAP info, local proof.
3. **AI Visibility** — clear services, FAQs, differentiators, structured content.
4. **Trust & Proof** — reviews, credentials, guarantees, photos, longevity.
5. **Follow-Up Readiness** — forms, booking paths, response promises, automation.
Plus: **The Revenue Leak Score** — all five roll into one 0–100 score with a
plain-English verdict.
