Our story

Started in a home kitchen. Perfected at 4am.

Golden Hour began as a Saturday farmers-market stand with one folding table and forty loaves that sold out in an hour. Three years later we have a brick oven, a small crew of bakers who genuinely argue about hydration percentages, and a line out the door on weekends.

Everything is mixed, shaped, and baked in-house. No freezer trucks, no par-baked anything. If it's in the case, someone here made it before sunrise.

— Maya & June, the bakers ♡
