The studio

Three disciplines, one landscape.

  • i. Design

    Site studies, planting plans, and 3D renderings. Every project begins with how you want to live outside — then we draw the garden around it.

  • ii. Build

    Our own crews set the stone, run the irrigation, and place every specimen agave by hand. One studio, one standard, no handoffs.

  • iii. Steward

    Seasonal pruning, drip audits, and replanting as the garden matures. A landscape is never finished — it's tended.

The process

From first walk to final planting.

The Site Walk

We spend an hour on your property reading sun, soil, and sightlines — and listening to how you want to use the space.

The Composition

Concept plans, a plant palette, and a fixed proposal. You'll see the garden before a single stone is placed.

The Build & Care

Our crew builds in weeks, not months. Then a seasonal stewardship plan keeps it thriving for years.
